Have you thought about the next weapon you wish to purchase? Broadswords are just scrap metal to you now. At Level 12 or 13 the gladius is an excellent choice for a sword, the dirk for a dagger, and the dwarven mace for a blunt weapon. You can find these weapons in stores, but they'll be expensive. You should be able to obtain a much better price by dealing with other adventurers.
I should also point out the short spear, a polearm that is well suited to these levels. Polearms are very slow to wield, but they allow you to attack several enemies at once. However, if you switch from your current weapon type to a polearm, expect some loss of combat effectiveness until you master the new weapon type.
