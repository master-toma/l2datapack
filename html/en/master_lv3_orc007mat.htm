You must pass individual tests to obtain marks.
The Mark of the Challenger is only given to those who pass the Trial of the Challenger. You must be above level 35 to take the test. And to take the Trial of the Challenger, visit Kash of Dion Castle Town.
The Mark of Glory is only given to those who pass the Testimony of Glory. You must be above level 37 to take the test. To take the Testimony of Glory, visit Prefect Vokian of Giran Castle Town.
And finally the Mark of the Duelist is only given to those who pass the Test of the Duelist. You must be above level 39 to take the test. To take the Test of the Dualist, meet Dualist Kaien of the Town of Oren.
