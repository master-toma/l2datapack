Let's talk about forming a party, a group of adventurers to hunt and travel with. Parties are created using Invite in the Action tab (ALT + C) of your Character Status window. First, click on the character you wish to invite into your party, then click the Invite icon. Next time you go hunting, you should try to form a party with another adventurer this way.
If you are having difficulty finding party members, use the Party Matching button in the Action tab of your Character Status window. This will display a list of adventurers who also want to form parties. You can use Invite, Whisper, or other useful commands from this window.
This is the point in your career where you decide whether you are a "tiger" who likes to hunt alone, or a "lion" who prefers the company of a group. If you're a tiger, you will need to perfect strategies to ensure your own survival, while a lion must learn how best to serve the interests of the group.
