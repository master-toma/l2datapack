Take your apprentice's spellbook, or whatever you are using, and head southeast of the village to hunt Kaboo Orcs, or Kaboo Orc grunts until you are ready to improve your weapons.
If you upgrade your weapons at level 9 or 10, you might venture farther to the southeast and cross the two bridges to find the region of the green dryads. If you travel southwest and across the river, you will find more Kaboo Orc grunts to challenge. Take care -- they are aggressive, and will attack you before you attack them, unlike most other monsters.
From level 10 to level 13, you will hunt in the areas where your quests take you. Hunt what monsters you can while completing such quests as the "Sea of Spores Fever" quest, or the "Skirmish with the Orcs" quest. You will be told where to go by the Elves who bestow the quests upon you.
