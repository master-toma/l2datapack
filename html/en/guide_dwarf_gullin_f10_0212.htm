One-handed blunt weapons are the traditional arms of us Dwarves. Their accuracy is higher than other weapon types, but their chance of inflicting critical damage is much lower. Blunt weapons reliably deliver consistent damage.
As you advance, you'll be able to learn skills that only work with blunt weapons. Something else that's interesting about blunt weapons -- they have a greater range of damage than other types. Say you have two different types of weapon with an average attack power of 10. The blunt weapon might do 7 to 13 points per attack, while the sword might do 9 to 11.
