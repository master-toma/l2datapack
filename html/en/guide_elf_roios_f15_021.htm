Have you considered the next weapon you wish to purchase? Broadswords are mere children's toys to you now. At Level 12 or 13 the gladius is an excellent choice for a sword, the dirk for a dagger, or the dwarven mace for a blunt weapon. You can find these weapons in stores, but they are very expensive. You should be able to obtain a much better price by dealing with other adventurers.
