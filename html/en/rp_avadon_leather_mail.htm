Creating this item requires a skill level that is one dimension higher than usual. The manufacturing of a valuable item such as this can sometimes end up in failure.

Manufacturing Process - I
Attempt to create the item with a rudimentary tool. When you use a rudimentary tool, you will have a higher chance of breaking the item during the manufacturing process. Regardless of whether you succeed or fail, the recipe will disappear.

Manufacturing Process - II
Attempt to create the item with a good tool. A high quality tool will lower the probability of breaking an item during its manufacturing process. Regardless of whether you succeed or fail, the recipe will disappear.
