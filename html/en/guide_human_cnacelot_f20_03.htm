By now, you're able to hunt anywhere on the island. I imagine spiders are your usual prey. There are plenty of spiders on the northern side of the island and on the mountain.
If you're in the mood for something different and more dangerous, you can try the Elven Ruins. For a long time nobody was allowed in there, but now it's open to adventurers. It's a dangerous place, but you shouldn't have a problem if you go with a party.
