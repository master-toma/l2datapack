What are the offensive skills that you are using already? Once you choose a skill, it is better to concentrate on developing that skill, than on acquiring new ones. After Level 10, you may increase your skills. Upgrade the ones you already use most.
As a rule, after you increase an offensive skill it is wise to learn supplemental skills which increase your abilities. If you have obtained Aura Defense, which increases your defense, learning Aura Attack, which increases your attack power, is a prudent choice.
And be sure to learn either Armor Mastery or Weapon Mastery. Which you choose depends on your tastes, but personally I would advise obtaining Weapon Mastery first. There is little enough difference, though, as long as you have one or the other.
