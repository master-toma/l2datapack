Obviously, the advantage of bows is their ability to deliver damage at range. There is more, though, that you should know. Bows are the slowest weapons, but they can inflict the greatest damage -- indeed, their damage can rival that of ranged spells.
A popular tactic is to open combat with a Power Shot, before you close to attack with your main weapon.
Most warriors use their bows as a backup weapon, but with proper training a bow can become your primary means of attack.
