Guard Leikan:
If you follow this road northeast, you will come to the Grim Ruins. Originally, it was the village of Bernice, where I was born, but it was reduced to ashes by the war. If you see its ghastly state, you will understand why it's now called the Grim Ruins.
If you pass the ruins and continue north, you will arrive at the forest where the Dark Elves live. Although I've never gone there myself... Travelers say that it is dark and eerie in a very unnatural way.
