When you've reached level 8, the most important thing is to acquire weapons. Consider the weapon you are wielding now, then think how you wish to improve it.
If you prefer spellcasting, buy an apprentice's spellbook. If you like armed combat better, buy a broadsword or mace.
At level 12, you should have enough money to purchase better weapons, but you might want to wait awhile, until you've prerformed a quest for the Chiefs. The quests often relate to weapons.
