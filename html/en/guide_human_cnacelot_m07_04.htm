When you reach level 7, you will learn new magic. Seek instruction from the Sorcerers of the Temple of Einhasad. The Temple of Einhasad is the largest building in the village, located on the south side of town.
You have probably noticed by now that when you defeat monsters you gain SP and experience. SP means "skill points," and they are consumed whenever you learn new skills or magic. You can check your current SP by clicking on your Character Status button, then on the Status tab.
