When fighting in a party, the Fighters have the job of standing face to face with the enemy. You can hurl attack spells, but it is equally important for you to support the Fighters with healing and strengthening spells.
To effectively support Fighters in combat, make sure you have Heal to at least level 2, and then enhance Might or Shield. Until you reach level 14, use Weapon Mastery to strengthen your Fighters. Heal or Battle Heal are more important in party combat than offensive spells.
If you wish to use attack magic, you must continue to develop your Wind Strike spell even after level 14. But remember that party combat only works as long as the Fighters are able to stand up to the enemy, so I advise you not to neglect Heal, Might and Shield.
