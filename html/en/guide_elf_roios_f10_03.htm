Travel southeast of the village and hunt Kaboo Orcs or Kaboo Orc grunts. Orcs are difficult to hunt, for they understand teamwork. In particular, beware the Kaboo Orc grunts, for they will attack you even before you attack them, unlike other monsters.
If you are past level 6, you may undertake the quest to "Hunt the Orcs" from Sentinel Rayen, which you should complete before you begin hunting farther afield for yourself.
You will find that the amulets and necklaces dropped by the orcs you slay will become a useful source of adena.
