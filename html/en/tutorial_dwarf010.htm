1st Change of Professions

At last you have reached level 19! Congratulations!
You may select your first professional upgrade at level 19. However, you must reach level 20 for the change to actually take effect, no matter which quest you are on.
A Dwarven Fighter may change to a Raider or an Artisan. For further information go visit the Head Blacksmiths or Warehouse Chiefs of the nearest village.
The Warehouse Chiefs are in charge of professional changes to a Raider. They can be found at warehouses. Head Blacksmiths are in charge of Artisans. They will be at the blacksmith shops.
