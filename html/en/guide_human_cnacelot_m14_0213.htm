One-handed blunt weapons have a higher accuracy than other weapons. However, their chance to inflict critical damage is much lower. Blunt weapons are designed to reliably deal predictable amounts of damage. This is the reason they are the first weapons a Mystic learns.
As you advance, you can acquire skills specifically for blunt weapons. Something to keep in mind about blunt weapons is that they have greater damage range than other weapon types. For example, a blunt weapon with an average damage of 10 will have a damage range of 7 to 13, whereas a sword with the same average power will have a range of 9 to 11.
