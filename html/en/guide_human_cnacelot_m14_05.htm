Many of the villagers could use your help, and there are plenty of quests out there for your level. They can be an excellent boost to your training.
Now Master Minia of the Fighter Guild is a very strange ... that is to say, a unique person. She has this hobby of collecting unique weapons, and she's been asking around for help. You could be of service.
Also, visit Grand Master Roien in Cedric's Training Hall. I'm told he recently received some valuable information from the library of Einhovant's School of Wizardry. I understand it involves a sword that figures in the histories of both the Humans and the Elves ... it sounds important. You should go get the whole story from him.
I think Grand Sorcerer Gallint of Einhovant's School could also use some help. Apparently the spirit of mirrors escaped during his research.
