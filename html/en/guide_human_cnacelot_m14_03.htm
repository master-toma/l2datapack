So, have you been to the Obelisk of Victory yet? It was built by the Elves and lies north of the village, to the left. It's in the middle of a nice hunting ground if you're around level 7. There are plenty of orcs and werewolves there. At your level you should be able to hold your own against orcs, orc fighters, and werewolves.
Just remember to be careful around orc fighters, they'll attack you before you attack them, unlike other monsters.
