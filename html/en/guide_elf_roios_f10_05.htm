Many of the villagers are in need of your help, and the quests they offer can benefit you as well. Do not neglect to seek them out.
At the southwestern gate, Sentinel Rayen is seeking volunteers to exterminate the orcs that have invaded the Elven Forest. Ah, I wish I could accompany you on this adventure.
Trader Herbiel, at the store on the west side of town, is collecting pouches of spores for research purposes. By all means speak to him.
Sentinel Kendell at the southeast gate is again recruiting warriors to battle against the orc tribes that came to our lands from over the Misty Mountains. This time, the plan is to subdue the chief commanders of the orcs.
