Have you encountered soulshot yet in your adventures?
It is a form of magical ammunition created by the Dwarves. You can use it like a potion while engaged in armed combat. It allows you to inflict greater damage, but it disappears when used.
Around Level 15 you will be able to acquire weapons that use soulshot. A soulshot rated better than x1 will increase the damage for the number of shots indicated.
