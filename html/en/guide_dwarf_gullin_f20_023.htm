Have you encountered soulshot yet in your adventures? You've heard me use the term before.
It is a form of magical ammunition devised by our Artisans many years ago. You can use it like a potion while engaged in armed combat. Soulshot allows you to inflict greater damage, but they disappear when used.
Around Level 15 you will be able to acquire weapons that use soulshot. A soulshot rated better than x1 will increase the damage for the number of shots indicated.
If you become an Artisan, you'll be able to make your own.
